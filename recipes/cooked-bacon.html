Oven Baked Bacon

Cooking bacon in the oven eliminates the need to tend a stove top, allows for more uniform cooking, and cooks larger batches at a time

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Line baking sheet with foil
  3. Arrange bacon on sheet with edges touching or slightly overlapping.
  4. Bake until crispy, about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Remove bacon from sheet and drain on paper towel-lined plate.
