Quick and easy home fries

Tasty potato breakfast I'll either undercook or burn

These home fries turn out crispy every time. They're great for breakfast or as a side dish. If you prefer crispy hash browns, use shredded potatoes (instead of cubed) and follow the recipe directions.

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse potato cubes with cold water, drain.
  2. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Place potatoes in skillet and season, mixing well
  4. Cover and cook for 10 minutes
  5. Remove lid and and cook for 10 more minutes, turning frequently.
